SMR Yatzy – Account and Data Deletion

SMR Yatzy users can delete their player account and related game data directly inside the app.

How to delete your data in the app

  1. Open the SMR Yatzy app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Wipe account or the account deletion option.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

This removes your player profile, saved player ID, nickname, scores, game statistics, and related game data stored for SMR Yatzy.

If you cannot access the app

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by email: sabata79@gmail.com

Please include your Player ID if available. If you do not know your Player ID, include your nickname and any details that may help identify your account.

Data retained

After account deletion, SMR Yatzy does not intentionally retain your player profile or game score data. Some technical records may remain temporarily in backups or third-party service logs according to their normal retention practices.

For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
